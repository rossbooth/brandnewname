# BrandNewName

Professional brand naming tool inspired by the methodology behind some of the world's most iconic brand names — Blackberry, Swiffer, Impossible Burger, Pentium, Febreze, Sonos, Microsoft Azure, SlimFast, Windsurf, and more.

## How to use

Run the naming process by invoking the skill:
```
/brandnewname
```

This launches a 6-phase interactive naming process:
1. **Strategic Discovery** — understand the product, category, and emotional territory
2. **Treasure Map** — design 3 parallel creative briefs and hunting territories
3. **Name Generation** — generate 150+ candidates across all territories
4. **Screening & Narrowing** — apply evaluation criteria (processing fluency, surprise, sound symbolism, competitive moat)
5. **Proof of Concept** — present finalists in context (headlines, taglines, introductions)
6. **Final Presentation** — top 3-5 names with full rationale and story

## Key Principles

- The AI runs the process but does NOT inject its own taste. All evaluation uses specific naming science criteria.
- Quantity leads to quality. Never stop generating at 50 names.
- The right name is polarizing. Comfort is the enemy.
- "Surprisingly familiar" = easy to process + unexpected in context.
- Competitors would never have the courage to use the right name.
